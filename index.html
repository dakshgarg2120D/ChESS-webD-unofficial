The Molecule
That Binds Us

Founded in 1999, ChESS is the heartbeat of the Chemical Engineering Department at IIT Roorkee. We act as the vital interface — a catalyst — between students and the experienced faculty, dissolving the barriers that form between them.

Through year representatives from B.Tech and M.Tech, we analyze student problems, hold faculty meetings, and engineer solutions. Selections are merit-based, powered by academic excellence and the collective support of our students.
